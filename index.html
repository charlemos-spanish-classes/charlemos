About Monica

I have been in Saskatoon for 18 years and I am originally from Ecuador. I come from a family of educators. My mom was an elementary English teacher and my dad a university engineering professor.

I started tutoring German at age 16, later earned my business degree and MBA, along with an English teaching certificate. I have taught English to kids and university students.

After moving to Canada, I began teaching Spanish at community associations and developed programs at Hampton Preschool. I also provide interpretation services for courts, schools, and medical appointments.

Teaching is not a job — it gives me joy. You will enjoy learning Spanish in a fun, dynamic way through songs, dialogues, and interactive activities.
